About

Hacking the University is a website of short interviews asking coders who work in universities about their craft.

Frequently asked questions

What is a Hacker?
I use the word fairly loosely. You might use the words 'coder', 'programer', or 'developer'. If you can relate to this description, then I'd say you are a Hacker. Sit proud!
Can I suggest someone to interview?
Yes please!
What's the background to all of this?
It relates to the work I do. I'm interested in how Hackers learn and their choice of tooling. Universities have been sites for hacking since the 1950s. I'd like to understand that relationship better. Read more over here.
Are you only interested in Hackers working in UK universities?
If you get paid to write code in a university, that's all that matters. It doesn't matter where you live.
Where are all the women who hack in universities?
I'm looking for them. Maybe you can help me?
